About Us

Research has made known that mediators make a profit of 100%-200% on Farm produce thereby leaving the farmers with peanut amount to fend on.

Also, almost 55% of farm produce perish before getting to the consumers because of the problem of middlemen transportation or lack of a medium of connection to potential customers/consumers

DirectFromFarm helps to alleviate some of these problems by providing a platform where farmers get customers even before their harvest date thereby solving the problem of a high percentage of the perishability of farm produce and also minimum extortion from middlemen, providing the farmers with the good profit for their work.
